About Young City Crew

My name is Neville Nyamongo, and I’m the founder of Young City Crew—a TEEN led dance movement based in Nairobi, Kenya. I started Young City in 2020, when I was just 11 years old, with one dream: to give children and teenagers a safe space to express themselves, grow their talents, and believe in their future.

Today, Young City Crew has grown into a strong and loving community with over 100 kids and two active hubs—one in **Kibera** and another in **Kabiria**. These hubs provide safe environments where young people meet to train, create, and support one another.

I’m currently being helped by other dedicated teenagers like me, who share the same passion and vision for change. Together, we run the program, guide the younger dancers, and create opportunities for them to learn, perform, and stay motivated. Our teamwork shows that young people can lead, inspire, and make a difference when given a chance.

We train together every week, Monday to Friday from 4 PM to 9 PM, building not just skill but confidence, focus, and discipline. On weekends, we often come together for performances, mentorship, and creative projects that strengthen teamwork, leadership, and unity.

What makes Young City special is that we don’t just dance—we teach discipline, love, and responsibility. Every member learns that talent is powerful, but it becomes truly meaningful when it’s used to inspire and uplift others. We believe that dance can change lives, open doors, and give hope to those who once felt forgotten.

Our dream: is to reach over 1,000 young people, offering them not just dance training but also mentorship, life skills, and support for education. We want to help them rise from struggle to strength and show them that they are capable of greatness.

Young City Crew stands for faith, family, and freedom—faith in God, the family we’ve built together, and the freedom to express who we are through art.

Our Mission

To empower young people through dance, mentorship, and education, giving them safe spaces to grow their talents, build confidence, and discover their purpose. We use art as a tool for change and hope.

Our Vision

To create a world where every young person, no matter their background, has the opportunity to rise and succeed. We dream of building one of the biggest Teens movements in Africa.

Our Core Values

Discipline: Consistent and dedicated.
Unity: One family, uplifting each other.
Faith: Belief in God's plan.
Creativity: Expressing and healing.
Leadership: Leading by example.
